Brendan Eich

Ceo

American programmer, creator of the JavaScript programming language. Since 1995 he worked for Netscape; participated in the founding of Mozilla, until 2014 was the chief engineer at the Mozilla Corporation. In March 2014, he was appointed CEO, but soon left Mozilla under pressure from dissatisfied with his appointment because of his support in 2008 to ban same-sex marriage. Currently he is the CEO of Brave Software.
